Privacy Policy

This Chrome extension does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.

The purpose of the extension is to automatically reject cookie consent banners by simulating a click on a "Reject" button when such a banner is detected on a webpage.

All processing is done locally in your browser. The extension does not send any information to remote servers and does not use cookies or tracking technologies.

If you have any questions or concerns, please contact us through our GitHub repository.
